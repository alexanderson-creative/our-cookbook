Fresh Pasta

A recipe by Julia

Prep 10 min
Rest 20 min
Cook 30 sec

Ingredients

  • 3 cups semolina flour (or all-purpose flour)
  • 4 extra large eggs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 50 ml water (adjust based on egg size)
  • Semolina flour for dusting

Fresh Pasta

Instructions

  1. Make the dough (by hand): Form a well with flour on a clean surface. Add eggs and olive oil to the center. Use your hands to scramble the eggs, then gradually incorporate flour until a ball forms. Add water as needed if the dough feels dry.
  2. Make the dough (food processor): Add all ingredients to a food processor with steel blade. Blend for 30 seconds. Add water if needed to bring it together.
  3. Knead: Knead the dough for about 5 minutes until smooth. It should be slightly firmer than pizza dough.
  4. Rest: Wrap the dough ball in plastic wrap and let rest for 15-20 minutes at room temperature.
  5. Flatten: Cut a 0.5-inch thick slice from the dough ball. Flatten with your hands and dust with semolina.
  6. Roll (setting 1): Set your pasta machine to the widest setting. Pass the dough through, fold it, and pass again. Repeat 4 times until smooth.
  7. Roll (settings 2-5): Gradually work through settings 5, 4, 3, and 2, passing the dough through once at each setting. Dust with semolina regularly to prevent sticking.
  8. Cut: Once the sheet is thin enough, cut into your desired shape using the pasta machine cutters or a knife.
  9. Cook: Drop pasta into boiling salted water. Fresh pasta cooks fast‚Äîjust 20-30 seconds. Drain and toss with your favorite sauce.

Notes

Semolina flour gives the pasta that perfect bite and helps it hold sauce better. You can use all-purpose flour if that's what you have.

Make ahead: The dough can be wrapped and refrigerated overnight. Just bring it to room temperature before rolling.

Sauce pairings: Fresh pasta pairs beautifully with simple sauces‚Äîbrown butter and sage, fresh tomato, or a light cream sauce.
